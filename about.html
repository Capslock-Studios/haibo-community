HAIBO-COMMUNITY

Building safer, more transparent communities with more opportunities for all.

HAIBO-COMMUNITY is a web-based platform aimed at enhancing community safety and development by leveraging community databases to provide access to information about known criminal activities. It enables users to report cases, register their details, and save information about their belongings, all without the need for account creation.

The platform identifies known community members using various identifiers to detect intruders, false identities, and negligent public servants. It also allows for anonymous reporting, automatic location and time capture, and offers full CRUD access for users to manage their reports and inventories, making it a comprehensive tool for maintaining and improving community security and awareness.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is HAIBO-COMMUNITY?

HAIBO-COMMUNITY is a web-based platform designed to enhance community safety and development by providing access to information about known criminal activities and enabling users to report cases and register their belongings.

How do I register my details on HAIBO-COMMUNITY?

To register, simply visit the registration page on the platform, fill in your personal details, and submit the form. No account creation is required.

Can I report a case anonymously?

Yes, you can report cases anonymously. The system will automatically capture your device's location and time without requiring any personal information.

What types of cases can I report?

You can report various types of cases, including theft, suspicious activities, and public safety hazards. The platform allows you to upload evidence and track the status of your reports.

How do I register my belongings on the platform?

Navigate to the inventory section, enter the details of your belongings, including unique identifiers and serial numbers, and save the information for future reference.

How are my reports and personal information protected?

We use advanced encryption methods to ensure your data is secure. Personal information is only accessible to authorized personnel.

Can I update or delete my reports and registered belongings?

Yes, you have full CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) access to your reports and registered belongings without needing to log in.

What should I do if I encounter a problem on the platform?

Visit the help center for troubleshooting tips or contact our support team via phone, email, or live chat for assistance.

Are there any costs associated with using HAIBO-COMMUNITY?

No, the platform is free to use for all community members.

How can I stay updated on new features and community alerts?

Follow our social media channels, subscribe to our newsletter, or check the platform regularly for updates and alerts.
